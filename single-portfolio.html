Overview

The Health Profession Opportunity Grants (HPOG), provides education and training for Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) recipients for healthcare occupations. Business Access is the Technical partner for two of the most successful HPOG programs in the country, ICATCH and Health Career Academy. We set out to create a marketing campaign that highlights Business Access's involvement within the programs, and offer our expertise as to how other companies may benefit from our services.


Client

Business Access Marketing Campaign

Role

UI/UX Design & Front End Developement

The Challenges facing our New site

In preparaation for our new marketing campiagn we discussed the ways in which our current website was working and the other areas in which it could be improved. Upon further analysis we discovered that the content and the overall layout of our current version were out of sync, which distraced our viewers from the main points we were trying to convey. So as we went back to the drawing boards, we discovered that we needed to create new content that was geared towards our audience and support our main points within a layout that reiforces our main objectives.

The Design Process & Solution

With the focus being on the content, my initial goal was to figure out a way in which the content would be the primary focus of the microsite. To acompllish this, I wire framed out ways in which the content would shine. But In the end it was obvious, that by taking a minimalist approach, we could achieve our first foremost onjective in making the content the sites centerpiece. The imagery and icons were added after the content was in place which were only used to reinforce our main objectives.
